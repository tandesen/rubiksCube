## Project Idea

Cubic is a content and commerce project centered on Rubik's cubes and group theory.

The content side:

- Publish videos explaining Rubik's cube ideas and group theory to a general audience.
- Cover 2x2 and 3x3 cubes.
- Keep public explanations intuitive and concrete.
- Make theoretical proof sections rigorous, detailed, and defensible even if the audience is smaller.

The commerce side:

- Sell Rubik's cube bundles connected to the course.
- 德森 has visited offline OEM / wholesale sources in Guangzhou.
- Observed wholesale cost: approximately RMB 6 per ordinary 3x3 cube.
- Planned product idea: print or mark numbers on a normal 3x3 cube, corresponding to notation in symmetric groups / permutation representations.
- Initial sale concept: RMB 38 bundle including 2x2 + 3x3 cubes, possible PDF course notes, and course links.

## Initial Mathematical Topics

The project should eventually cover:

- How many possible states a 3x3 Rubik's cube has.
- Why a legal 3x3 cube move sequence cannot swap only two pieces while leaving all other pieces fixed.
- God's number and the claim that every 3x3 position can be solved in a bounded number of moves. Do not publish "19 moves" as the general 3x3 God's number; current source notes point to 20 in half-turn / face-turn metric and 26 in quarter-turn metric.
- The relation between cube moves, permutations, parity, orientations, and group actions.
- How the 2x2 cube provides a cleaner first model before the 3x3.
